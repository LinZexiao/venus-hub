### Phase 1&2 (end)

Phase 1 and Phase 2 of Venus SP Incubator was started on July 8, 2021 running until Feb.6 2022. 

There was a prerequisite for SPs which required storage providers to have a minimum sealing power to support at least 1TiB/day. There were 97 applications we received during past two phases, 22 SPs have been incubated in the program.
