### Phase 1 (end)

Venus Master Phase 1 started from Sept. 20, 2021 running until Feb.6, 2022. To be a Venus Master, participant should continuously contributes to the development of the Filecoin network with full experience in coding, mining or service. Selected Masters will provide consulting services and technical support to the storage providers in the Incubation Center, ensure these participants to expand the distributed storage pool service in the Filecoin network.### Results
